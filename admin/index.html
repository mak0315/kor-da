How Payments Flow
1
Guest Pays via Safepay
Guest pays PKR via card, EasyPaisa, or JazzCash. Safepay processes and holds payment in your merchant escrow account.
2
Money Held in Escrow
Payment stays in Safepay escrow. Neither you nor the host can touch it yet. Guest is protected until check-in.
3
Guest Checks In → 24hr Window
Guest arrives. 24-hour dispute window begins. If no dispute filed, funds release automatically at the 24hr mark.
4
Automatic Split & Payout
Safepay sends 92% to host bank/EasyPaisa/JazzCash, and 8% commission to your Kor Da merchant account. Both get confirmation SMS.
💳

Payment records will appear here once you connect your Safepay merchant account. See Settings to configure.
