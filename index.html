FAQ

Common Questions

How do I pay? Can I use EasyPaisa?
+
Yes — EasyPaisa (03495620844) is our primary payment method. We also accept JazzCash, debit/credit cards, and bank transfer. All payments in PKR through Safepay escrow (SBP-licensed). No USD, no foreign fees.
How does Safepay escrow protect me?
+
Your payment goes into Safepay escrow — SBP-licensed — and is held until 24 hours after you check in and confirm your stay. If you dispute within 24 hours, the money stays in escrow until we resolve it. Hosts cannot receive payment before you're satisfied.
How are hosts CNIC-verified?
+
Every host's CNIC is verified against NADRA's official Verisys database before their listing goes live. Only a registered Pakistani company can access Verisys — this is one of Kor Da's key competitive advantages over foreign platforms like Airbnb.
Which areas of Islamabad are covered?
+
All Islamabad sectors: F-5 to F-11 (including all sub-sectors), G-6 to G-15, E-7, E-11, I-8, I-10, H-8 to H-13, all DHA phases (1–3, Valley), all Bahria Town phases (1–7, Enclave), Blue Area, Diplomatic Enclave, Bani Gala, Gulberg Greens, Park Enclave, and Top City-1. Rawalpindi including Saddar, DHA, and Bahria Town Rawalpindi is also covered.
What commission does Kor Da charge?
+
Kor Da charges hosts 9% only on completed bookings — the lowest in Pakistan. Airbnb charges 14–16%. Zero listing fee, zero monthly cost. You keep 91%, paid via EasyPaisa (03495620844), JazzCash, or bank transfer after guest checks in.
What if something is wrong with my stay?
+
File a dispute within 24 hours of check-in via WhatsApp (+97471259576). We respond same day. If the property doesn't match the listing, you get a full refund from escrow. Both parties submit evidence and we make a fair, binding decision.
When does Kor Da launch in Islamabad?
+
We are in final onboarding of our first verified hosts in Islamabad — launching very soon. Join the waitlist to be first notified and receive 10% off your first booking. Founding hosts get zero commission for the first 3 months.
