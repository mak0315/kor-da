Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Kor Da (Pvt.) Ltd.
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it. We do not sell your data to anyone.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Kor Da:

  • Account information: Name, email address, WhatsApp/phone number
  • Identity verification: CNIC number (for hosts, verified via NADRA Verisys)
  • Booking information: Dates, property, number of guests
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Safepay. We do not store card numbers.
  • Communications: Messages sent through our platform chat system
  • Usage data: Pages visited, search queries, device type

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage bookings and payments
  • To verify host identity through NADRA Verisys
  • To send booking confirmations and important updates
  • To resolve disputes between guests and hosts
  • To improve our platform and prevent fraud
  • To send marketing emails (you can opt out at any time)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • NADRA: For host CNIC verification (identity check only)
  • Safepay: For secure payment processing
  • Hosting providers: For platform infrastructure
  • Law enforcement: When legally required

4. Platform Chat & Communication Policy

All communication between guests and hosts must remain on our platform until a booking is confirmed. Our chat system monitors for and blocks sharing of personal phone numbers, email addresses, and off-platform contact details. This protects both parties' booking and payment rights.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. Payment data is handled entirely by Safepay's PCI-compliant infrastructure. CNIC data is used only for verification and stored encrypted.

6. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Correct inaccurate information

To exercise these rights, email: ah5909931@gmail.com

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only — for login sessions and security. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

Kor Da is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify registered users of any significant changes to this policy via email.
